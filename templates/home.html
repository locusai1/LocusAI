FAQ

Questions, answered

Everything you need to know before you start your free trial.

Does it actually sound human?
Yes. LocusAI uses a natural British voice and real-time conversation, so callers get a warm, professional experience — not a clunky phone menu. It greets repeat callers by name and handles back-and-forth questions just like a person would.
Is setup really only 10 minutes?
For most businesses, yes. Our onboarding wizard walks you through your services, hours, and how you'd like calls handled. There's nothing to install — if you can fill in a form, you can set up LocusAI. You can refine everything later from your dashboard.
What happens if the AI can't answer something?
It escalates to you. LocusAI recognises when a caller is frustrated or asks something outside its knowledge, and seamlessly hands off — by taking a message, sending you an alert, or transferring the call to your team. You stay in control of the important moments.
Will it work with my calendar and tools?
LocusAI integrates with the tools you already use — including Google Calendar and Calendly — so bookings land where you expect them. We believe in working with your stack, not forcing you to switch everything.
How does pricing work? Any per-minute charges?
Never. You pay one flat monthly rate — no per-minute call charges, no surprise overages. Every plan starts with a 14-day free trial and no credit card, and you can cancel anytime.
Are calls recorded and GDPR-compliant?
Calls begin with a clear recording notice, as required under UK law, and your data is handled in line with UK GDPR. You can review full transcripts and recordings from your dashboard at any time.

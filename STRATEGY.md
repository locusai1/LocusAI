# LocusAI Strategy: Wedge → Expand → Platform

**Date Established:** January 2026
**Core Insight:** Businesses don't switch their entire operating system, but they WILL add something new that solves a painful gap.

---

## The Hard Truth About Business Platforms

Businesses DON'T switch their entire operating system easily.

They already have:
- Square/Stripe for payments
- Google Calendar or Calendly for scheduling
- Some spreadsheet or basic CRM
- Their accountant's preferred system

Asking them to replace ALL of that? That's a 6-month sales cycle, massive onboarding, data migration headaches, staff retraining. Most SMBs won't do it unless they're starting fresh.

## What Businesses WILL Do

**Add something new that solves a painful gap.**

An AI that answers their phone 24/7? That's not replacing anything - they don't have that. It's **additive**.

| Switching (Hard Sell) | Adding (Easy Sell) |
|-----------------------|-------------------|
| "Replace your calendar" | "Never miss a call again" |
| "Move your payments to us" | "AI books appointments while you sleep" |
| "Import all your customers" | "Works with your existing calendar" |

---

## The Three-Phase Strategy

### PHASE 1: THE WEDGE (Year 1)
**Goal: Best AI Receptionist on the market. Period.**

The pitch: "Your phone rings at 2am. Your competitor's AI answers. You lose the customer."

Key characteristics:
- Solves ONE painful problem brilliantly
- Zero switching cost - ADDS to their existing stack
- Integrates with what they already use
- Dead simple setup (10 minutes to go live)

**Focus Areas:**
1. Caller recognition - "Hi Sarah!" (immediate wow factor)
2. Real-time availability - Actually books correctly
3. Integrations - Google Calendar, Calendly, Acuity (work with their tools)
4. Multi-channel - Voice + SMS + Chat widget
5. Voice quality - Natural, human-like, handles interruptions
6. Multi-language - Spanish as priority

**What we DON'T build yet:**
- Our own payment processing
- Complex CRM features
- Inventory management
- Staff scheduling
- Marketing automation

### PHASE 2: THE HOOK (Year 2)
**Goal: Strategic integrations + optional LocusAI native features**

Once they love the AI receptionist:
- Offer scheduling that "works better with the receptionist"
- Offer CRM that "tracks everyone who calls automatically"
- Offer payments "so customers can pay when they book"

Key principle: **Always integrate first, offer native second.**

If they use Google Calendar → integrate beautifully
If they want better → "Try our scheduling, it's built for the AI"

### PHASE 3: THE PLATFORM (Year 3+)
**Goal: Full business OS for those ready to consolidate**

By now:
- Customers trust us
- They're using multiple LocusAI features
- Switching cost works in OUR favor
- We've earned the right to be their platform
